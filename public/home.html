About QuikBuzz

QuikBuzz allows you to create a quiz room, invite your friends, and compete in real-time. Once a room is created, you can join it by entering the room code. Teams can buzz in to answer questions, and the app will keep track of scores and answers. Enjoy the challenge and have fun! This buzzer has a security feature, where hosts are informed when participants switch their tabs.
